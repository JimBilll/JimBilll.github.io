Through the second half of 2024 I completed my MEng industrial placement at IPTV infrastructure company VITEC in Dalgety Bay. During this time I worked on the UI for Prism, a transcoder product.

Well Formed

GitHub Dissertation

Well Formed is my dissertation project. It is a web application for generating HTML form elements with accessibility features built in. This allows for the creation of WCAG compliant web forms without knowledge of the standards.

Article

In summer 2023 I completed a ten week internship at CAS in Edinburgh. I was tasked with adding accessibility features to their product WorkPro. For this I learned about accessibility requirements and how to implement them.
